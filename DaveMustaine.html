David Scott Mustaine is an American musician best known as the co-founder, vocalist, lead guitarist, and primary songwriter of American heavy metal band Megadeth. Prior to forming Megadeth, Mustaine was the lead guitarist of American heavy metal band Metallica. Dave's Musical Discography is now 20 Albums long, His first being Metallica's Demo tape, No Life 'Til Leather in 1982

Born: 13 September 1961 (age 58 years), La Mesa, California, United States
