Kiko Loureiro

Follow @kikoloureiro

Pedro Henrique "Kiko" Loureiro is a Brazilian guitarist, best known as a member of heavy metal bands Angra and Megadeth. Loureiro is known for his tremendous technical skill on the guitar, frequently incorporating Multiple complex techniques in the same run or phrase. He is also well known for both his instructional and demonstration videos as well as for writing columns for and appearing on the cover of magazines Cover Guitarra, Guitar & Bass, and Young Guitar.

Born: 16 June 1972 (age 48 years), Rio de Janeiro, State of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
